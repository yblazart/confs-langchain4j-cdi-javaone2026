The answer:

@RegisterAIService

CDI bridges LangChain4j and your server

"Inject an AI agent as naturally as an EntityManager"

The answer fits in one annotation: @RegisterAIService. CDI already injects everything in your apps. EntityManager, DataSource, REST clients... so why not an AI agent? That's exactly what we built. A CDI extension that turns an annotated interface into an injectable bean, wired to LangChain4j under the hood. Same patterns you already know, brand new capabilities.
